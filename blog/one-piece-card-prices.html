PSA 10 vs Raw: Should You Grade?

Grading makes sense for high-end cards, but it's not free money.

When to Grade:

  • Cards worth $100+ raw (justifies $50 grading cost)
  • Alt arts and manga panels (biggest PSA premiums)
  • Cards pulled fresh from pack (centered, pristine)

When to Skip Grading:

  • Cards under $50 raw (grading eats profit)
  • Visible edge wear or surface scratches
  • Off-center cuts (automatic 8 or lower)
Grading Math:
Cost: $50 (PSA) + shipping + insurance
Break even: Card must sell for $100+ more graded
Risk: 30% of cards get PSA 9 (minimal premium)
